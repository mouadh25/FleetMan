# Pricing, Packaging & Marketing Strategy
**Market Positioning:** Maintenance-First Fleet Management MVP for Algeria

## 1. The "Blue Ocean" Market Persona & Global Validation
Our target client is NOT looking for live GPS dots on a map. They are tired of paying expensive monthly fees for GPS trackers that do nothing to stop engines from blowing up or drivers from hiding broken mirrors.

**Global Validation of "Non-GPS" Fleet SaaS:**
This is not a theoretical model. Globally, pure-play "Maintenance & eDVIR" platforms (that do not require hardware or GPS tracking) are multi-billion dollar companies. Platforms like **Fleetio**, **Whip Around**, and **AUTOsist** thrive by providing digital checklists, parts management, and financial TCO tracking without moving a single dot on a map. We are bringing this exact, proven SaaS model exclusively to Algeria.

**The Ideal Client Persona (ICP):**
*   **Fleet Size:** 10 to 50 Vehicles (SMB to Mid-Market).
*   **Industry:** Construction, FMCG Distribution, Agriculture, Medical Transport, or Local Delivery.
*   **The Buyer:** The CEO (Owner) or the Financial Director (DAF) who is tired of bleeding cash on blind repairs.

## 2. The Sales Pitch & Handling the "GPS Objection"
When you pitch this to an Algerian CEO, they will inevitably ask: 
*"Why shouldn't I just buy a GPS tracker? Every other transport company just uses GPS."*

**The Ultimate Rebuttal (The Sales Pitch):**
*"A GPS tracker is a camera looking backward. FleetMan is a financial shield looking forward. 
A GPS tells you where your truck died on the highway. We tell you why it’s going to die next week—and we stop it from leaving the yard.
You pay 15,000 DZD for a GPS to stop a driver from stealing 2,000 DZD of diesel. You pay FleetMan to stop a driver from destroying a 500,000 DZD engine block because they ignored the oil light. GPS manages locations; we manage your cash flow."*

## 3. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy & Outreach Methods

### Method 1: The "ROI Audit" (Direct Value Selling)
Do not sell software; sell financial audits. 
*   **The Approach:** Contact the DAF or CEO. Offer a free 30-minute "Fleet Leakage Audit".
*   **The Execution:** Sit down with an Excel sheet. Ask: "How many engines did you replace last year? How many fines did you pay for expired Contrôle Technique? How many times did you buy a part locally at a 40% markup because it wasn't in your warehouse?"
*   Calculate the exact DZD amount lost (e.g., 3,000,000 DZD). Then pitch the software as a fraction of that cost. The software pays for itself in month 2.

### Method 2: Insurance Broker Leverage
Insurance companies despise claims stemming from poor maintenance. 
*   **The Approach:** Partner with massive corporate insurance brokers (SAA, CAAT, CASH).
*   **The Execution:** You give the broker a cut, or the broker offers your software at a discount to their clients. If a fleet uses FleetMan, the insurance company knows the trucks are undergoing daily eDVIRs, reducing the risk of catastrophic accidents caused by bald tires or bad brakes. This creates a massive, trusted distribution channel.

### Method 3: LinkedIn & Tradeshow Interception
*   **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Target exact job titles: `Directeur Logistique`, `Responsable Parc Auto`, `Directeur Général` within the Algiers, Oran, and Setif industrial zones.
*   **Physical Tradeshows:** Put a simple booth at local exhibitions like SITP (Travaux Publics) or Logistical. Put a dirty, rugged tablet on a stand to demonstrate the "Kiosk" feature. Hand the tablet to construction CEOs and let them see how easily a 60-year-old driver can use it.

## 4. SaaS Pricing Strategy (B2B Algerian Tiering)
Do not charge "Per User". The entire philosophy relies on every employee (Drivers, Mechanics, Managers) using the system. Charge by the physical asset being protected.

**1. The "Essential" Plan (The GPS Undercutter)**
*   **Target:** 5–15 Vehicles.
*   **Price:** 1,000 DZD per vehicle / month (Billed Annually).
*   **Market Positioning:** Standard GPS tracking in Algeria costs 1,000 to 1,500 DZD/month *plus* a 15,000 DZD hardware installation fee. This plan mathematically undercuts them. It requires ZERO hardware and immediately solves the daily eDVIR, Issue Tracking, and Legal Document Expirations.

**2. The "Professional" Plan (The Core MVP)**
*   **Target:** 15–50 Vehicles.
*   **Price:** 1,500 DZD per vehicle / month (Billed Annually).
*   **Market Positioning:** Matches premium GPS subscription prices. However, instead of just showing a blinking dot on a map, this delivers the full RBAC Ecosystem, Pre-Trip Geofencing, Predictive Maintenance Alerts, Vendor Scoring, and the CEO Financial Dashboard.

**3. The "Enterprise / Self-Hosted" Plan**
*   **Target:** 50+ Vehicles or Government/Para-Statal fleets.
*   **Price:** Custom Pricing (Heavy upfront implementation fee + Annual Maintenance Contract).
*   **Included:** Dedicated local server deployment (On-Premise or ICOSNET), dedicated account manager.
